Nature of Manifold

Ideally, the manifold should be one dimensional since only one parameter is required to describe different images in space, that is the angle by which the head rotates.But the data may not necessarily be linear. Isomap does non linear dimensionality reduction for the dataset and represents the data very well in two dimensional embedding retaining the similarities among various points. Similar looking points are close to each other and it is well seperated from the points that are far apart. We can see three different line segments such that along one of them, we have points when head is straight, one captures the the points where head is turned left while along the other line we have points where the head is turned right. Thus isomap gives a good two dimensional embedding for the given high dimensional data.

Discussion

From the graphs of the lower dimensional embedding for various kernel in kernel PCA, it is clear that rbf kernel does not give good results and different looking points in high dimension cannot be distinguished in low dimension.

While in case of linear, cosine, and polynomial kernels, the lower dimensional embedding captures the similarity among various points.It is also evident from the KNN(K nearest neighbours) results that similarities between points is retained in the lower dimensional embedding during Kernel PCA using the above mentioned kernels.
